Product Terms

Blank Canvas

The ‘Blank Canvas’ product is perfect for creating something completely bespoke on a CAT-A floor. The only cost here would be your rent - the rest is down to you!

  • Monthly rent payment only
  • Manage and procure the design and build yourself
  • Manage the space and suppliers yourself

Fitted

The ‘Fitted’ product is available for all units. For CAT-A spaces, the design and build is managed for you. Fitted spaces can be tailored to reflect your brand.

  • Design and build included
  • No design and build costs upfront
  • Manage the space and suppliers yourself

Managed

The ‘Managed’ product is available for all units. This option is an all-inclusive package, perfect for those wanting everything taken care of

  • All-inclusive package with expert design, fit-out and management
  • Fully managed
  • One simple fixed monthly price
